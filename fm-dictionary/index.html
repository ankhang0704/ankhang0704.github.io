Core Features

Everything you need
to master vocabulary

Roadmap Learning

Structured learning path with chapters, stages, and progressive difficulty. Track your journey from beginner to advanced.

AI Pronunciation

Real-time pronunciation checking with AI. Audio is processed securely via Cloudflare Workers and deleted immediately to ensure privacy.

Achievement System

8 unique achievement badges and daily streaks to keep you motivated. All progress is securely synced via Firebase.

Social Learning

Join private study groups to compete on leaderboards with friends and colleagues, boosting professional learning motivation.

FM Dictionary

Look up 1,500+ Facility Management terms with detailed definitions, real-world examples, and full offline capability.

Multilingual UI

Full interface localization in Vietnamese and English via easy_localization — including badges, labels, and all system text.

App Permissions

What the app accesses
and exactly why

Microphone

Used exclusively for the pronunciation practice feature. Audio is processed on-device in real-time for comparison with reference audio. No recording is stored, transmitted, or retained after the session ends.

Required for feature

Internet Access

Syncs learning progress to Firebase, enables Google / Apple sign-in, and fetches content updates. The core learning experience works fully offline once content is downloaded.

Required for sync

Push Notifications

Sends optional daily learning reminders and streak alerts to keep you on track. Can be disabled at any time in your device Settings without affecting any App functionality.

Optional

About the Project

Removing language barriers
in Vietnam's FM industry

FM Dictionary is a premium educational application developed to remove language barriers in the Facility Management industry. All professional content and terminology are owned and compiled by Thuy Ta.

The app was designed and developed by An Khang Studio, representing a complete production-grade Flutter project: clean architecture, offline-first design, and modern gamified learning mechanics.
