1. Introduction

FM Dictionary ("the App", "we", "our") is developed by An Khang Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application, available on iOS and Android.

By using FM Dictionary, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information Collection

  • / Account information — Name, email, and photo from Google or Apple sign-in.
  • / Learning progress — Lessons, quiz scores, streaks, and badges. Synced to servers for multi-device support.
  • / Audio recordings (temporary) — Real-time analysis for pronunciation. Discarded immediately. No storage or logging.
  • / Study Groups — Name and progress metrics visible only to members of the same group.
Zero Access: We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or browsing history.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while active. Upon deletion request, all personal info (name, email) is permanently removed within 30 days. Learning progress is anonymized for aggregate analytics. Local databases (Hive) are deleted when the App is uninstalled.
